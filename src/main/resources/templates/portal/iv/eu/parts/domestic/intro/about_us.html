Doosan Industrial Vehicle (DIV) has established as a new corporation since Forklifts division was separated from Doosan Infracore on July, 2011. And we have started to have DIV’s own Parts Distribution Center(PDC) since 16th of April, 2012.

DIV PDC is located in Incheon Aham Logistics Park which is 12km away from Incheon International Airport. It’s much closer than the previous PDC in Ansan.

We are trying to have stronger competitiveness with DIV’s own PDC collaborating with DIV’s overseas PDCs in Belgium, United Kingdom, USA and China.

We have the capability to stock over 80,000 SKU with 20,700 sq meters to ensure a stable supply of all service parts of DIV’s Forklift. Throughout our world-class services and global PDC network, we are fully dedicated to provide over 95% parts fill rate to more than 130 dealers in the world.

Since December in 2008, PDC has equipped with PDA storage system and Unmanned parts vertical transporting system. Also, SAP ERP system applied to all parts operation system in order to provide world class service and operational excellence.

Over 40 warehouse workers are dedicated to ensure efficient full-service parts operations at all times, from receiving to picking up, packing and shipment.

The state-of-the-art parts management system based on SAP ERP in Master PDC can handle thousands of orders from dealers and regional PDCs and send thousands of shipments in the fast way every month. DIV Parts On-Line System (Doobiz / http://doobizplus.doosan-iv.com) is available to DIV dealers via the internet. Doobiz gives convenient user interface and the most updated parts information for all DIV Forklifts. And Doobiz provides electronic parts catalogues of all DIV Forklifts with easy navigation of parts numbers and illustrations.
